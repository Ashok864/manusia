what we do

MiDAgS™️ - (ManUsIA™️ intelligent Digital Agricultural Systems)

MiDAgS™️ assists farmers and plantation owners in digitising and automating their entire plantation processes from planting, watering, fertilising, weeding, harvesting and transportation to the processing facilities. Our in-house designed and built Autonomous Harvesting, Fertilising, Weeding, and Transportation (Auto-HaFeWeT) solution is able to reduce costs while increasing yield with little to no upfront costs.

MiAMS™️ - (ManUsIA™️ intelligent Aerospace Management Systems)

MiAMS™️ helps ease the tedious and repetitive processes involved in the Aerospace industry by adapting Robotic Process Automation (RPA) to ensure safety and quality is upheld at all times. When coupled with autonomous Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS), MiAMS provides wholesome planning, documentation, flight operations, resource management, business management and business analysis as well as reporting to keep your operations flight ready all day, every day.

MiRAB™️ - (ManUsIA™️ intelligent Rural Area Broadband)

MiRAB™️ provides seamless telecommunication services in areas lacking connectivity for your day to day activities as well as your business needs. Agriculture, Farming, Fisheries, Mining . construction and many more areas without any telecommunication services can take advantage MiRAB™️. Ad-hoc networks can also be set up whenever necessary such as in disaster affected areas. Never be out of coverage again.

MiSSy™️ - (ManUsIA™️ - Intelligent Security & Surveillance Systems)

MiSSy™️ is your one stop solution for all your autonomous intelligent security and surveillance needs. Combining Robotic Process Automation (RPA) with Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS) has lead to this wholesome solution at a fraction of typical costs. Golf courses, vast plantations, and sprawling warehouses can incorporate MiSSy into their arsenal of security and surveillance tools.
